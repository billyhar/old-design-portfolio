Digital Designer and Creative Enthusiast

I have always been fascinated by technology from a young age and I am always looking forward to new advancements in the tech world. I became interested in web design through taking a course in basic HTML and CSS almost three years ago. From there I have developed my skills to utilise complex front-end tools such as JQuery, Bootstrap and Flex-box. More recently I have taken an interest in mobile app design creating UI mockups for new apps. As well as university projects, I have also worked on a couple of mobile apps in my own time. I have also begun teaching myself Swift via Lynda.com and Youtube. Most of all I enjoy developing new ideas to help fix current problems. To make people's experience with the online world as efficient as possible and create a nice aesthetically pleasing web environment for everyone to use. In my spare time I enjoy skateboarding and snowboarding. I am looking for a job that challenges my skills as a designer and creative. One that not only challenges them but improves my skills at the same time. My ultimate goal is to work on a project that will really make a difference in the way that people use the internet and mobile devices.

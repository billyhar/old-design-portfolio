GoBowls - Complete Website Build

This was a team based project, which involved myself and 3 other classmates from university. It involved a real life client proposing an idea for us to design, develop build and deploy. The clients business idea was to create a website where lawn bowling club members and event organisers could collaborate in the same place to create and register for specific lawn bowling events throughout New Zealand. As a team we managed to collaborate to produce a highly refined website that is now able to be used by the general public.
My specific roles in this project involved: redirecting and managing the hosting of the site, database and wordpress platform. Designing the user interfaces and user flow of the site to be developed and working on the development of the event registration and event hosting sections of the site. I was also responsible for directing the team in the right direction by planning out milestones and SCRUM sprints to get the site finished by the deadline. The site also uses the Wordpress CMS to manage user accounts, event registration and posting and themes.

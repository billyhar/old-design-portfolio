Victoria University of Wellington - School of Design Website UI Proposal

This was a project which I completed in October 2016. I was tasked by my head tutor to design a user interface for the proposed School of Design website. I gave myself a brief and laid out the content that I was going to need. I then proceeded creating a colour scheme, fonts and icon library. Then following on and doing some research for my target audience. I then designed 4 client personas of typical people who would be using the site. I then designed a journey map of the site and showed a particular course the person would take. I then created a basic sitemap to layout the structure of the proposed website. I then proceeded to start designing basic wireframes of different pages of the site. This then progressed into more detailed wireframes. I then repeated this phase by designing mobile responsive UI's.
